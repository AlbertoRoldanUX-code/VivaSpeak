VivaSpeak ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our website, contact us, or book a demo consultation.

By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

When you fill out our contact form, we collect:

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
