Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is VivaSpeak?

VivaSpeak is an AI-powered voice assistant that handles inbound and outbound calls for businesses. It automates customer interactions, manages reservations, answers FAQs, and more.

How does VivaSpeak work?

VivaSpeak uses AI to understand and respond to customer inquiries. It can handle calls in multiple languages, integrate with your CRM, and provide 24/7 support.

What are the benefits of VivaSpeak?

VivaSpeak streamlines customer interactions, reduces wait times, automates bookings, and boosts sales with outbound calls. It's a cost-effective solution for businesses of all sizes.

What industries can benefit from VivaSpeak?

VivaSpeak is ideal for restaurants, clinics, hotels, e-commerce, and service-based businesses that handle a high volume of customer calls.

Does VivaSpeak require any additional hardware?

No, VivaSpeak is a cloud-based solution, so it does not require any extra hardware. It can be integrated with your existing phone system and CRM.

How easy is it to integrate VivaSpeak with my current systems?

VivaSpeak offers seamless integration with popular CRMs, booking platforms, and business tools. Our team can assist with a smooth setup process.

What languages does VivaSpeak support?

VivaSpeak supports multiple languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, and more. Contact us for a full list of supported languages.

Can VivaSpeak make outbound sales calls?

Yes, VivaSpeak can be used for outbound sales, appointment reminders, and follow-up calls to engage customers and improve conversion rates.

Is VivaSpeak available 24/7?

Yes, VivaSpeak operates 24/7, ensuring that your business never misses a customer inquiry, even outside regular business hours.

How can I get started with VivaSpeak?

To get started, simply book a demo or contact us. Our team will guide you through the process and help you select the best plan for your needs.
